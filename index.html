Why should you publish your books on Author's World?

We pride ourselves in being the world's largest book publishing tool. In addition to helping you publish our own books, we also offer:

Best Pice Recommendations

With one simple search we connect you to various companies buying used books online. We ensure you receive the best prices for your books. Shipping is free and you're often paid the same day your book is received.

Reader Ratings & Reviews

After you've searched your ISBN, we display a list of companies with offers for your book. Author's World users can rate and review each reader with our feedback system. We display this rating next to each reader so you can sell your books with confidence.

Other Buyback Resources

Have questions about selling books online? Can't find the ISBN? Will your book be accepted in its current condition? Is your book counterfeit? We have many tips available whether this is your first time selling books, or if you have your own book buying business.
